Start

Firstly, generate your user_id and user_token by navigating to the provided link and submitting your information. If you receive a success message, check your email for your user_id and user_token. LINK: texttomorseconverter.onrender.com/api/generate_credentials?email=YOUR_EMAIL

Keep your credentials in the safe place!

Your credentials will expire after one month, after that you need do this whole process again.

Using API

After that just simply navigate to that link and fill it with your freshly generated credentials. LINK: texttomorseconverter.onrender.com/api/generate_data?user_id=YOUR_USER_ID&user_token=YOUR_USER_TOKEN&user_input=YOUR_INPUT
